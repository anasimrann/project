Know About Me

Hello, my name is Anas Bin Imran and I am a computer science student in my final semester at FAST University, which is considered to be the number one computer science university in Pakistan. However, after some time, I realized that my true interest lay in computer science, and I made the difficult decision to switch my focus and prepare for the test to get admission into FAST University. I was determined to succeed and worked hard to prepare for the test, eventually earning admission to the university. Over the past few years, I have had the opportunity to explore different areas of computer science, from programming languages and software development to algorithms and data structures. I have also had the chance to work on a number of exciting projects, including a mobile application that I developed as part of my coursework and an internship I completed at a local software development company. Looking towards the future, I am excited to continue exploring the world of computer science and leveraging my skills and knowledge to tackle new challenges and create innovative solutions.
